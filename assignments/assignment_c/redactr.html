Welcome to SafeText Scrambler! This web app helps you securely share content on social media by scrambling portions of text you prefer to keep private. Simply enter your text and specify the words you want to scramble. After clicking "Redact Now", the app will scan through your text and replace the specified words with your chosen replacement.
